Ceviche sole

From the Peruvian coast to the world. Ceviche is one of the delicacies of our Peruvian cuisine, distinguished by the variety of fish and seafood we have in the region, which facilitates the diversity of its presentations, textures, and flavors.

Ingredients

  • 300g sole filet
  • 1 red onion
  • 3 lemons
  • salt
  • peper

Instructions

  1. Cut the sole into equal-sized cubes.
  2. In a bowl, place the fish and add the julienned onion.
  3. Squeeze the lemon juice and add it to the bowl with the fish and onion.
  4. Add salt to taste.
  5. Add a pinch of pepper to the bowl and mix all the ingredients well.
  6. Before continuing, make sure the bowl or container you are using is very cold to maintain the freshness of the ingredients.
  7. To serve, place the ceviche mixture on a plate accompanied by a lettuce leaf.
  8. Add slices of sweet potato or potato around the ceviche.
  9. Add a handful of sweet corn on top of the ceviche.
  10. Optionally, you can add canchita serrana (toasted corn) according to your preference.
  11. If you like spicy food, you can add rocoto chili to taste to give the ceviche a touch of flavor and spice.
  12. Serve the sole ceviche and enjoy!
